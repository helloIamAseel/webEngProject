This Week’s Recipes

Our selected recipes for this week are easy to prepare, nutritious, and full of flavor.

Crisp Garden Salad

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 2 cups romaine lettuce (chopped), 1 cucumber (sliced), 1 tomato (diced), ¼ red onion (thinly sliced), 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt, black pepper

Instructions: In a salad bowl, combine all chopped vegetables. In a small jar, shake olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to emulsify. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss just before serving for a fresh, vibrant taste.

Notes & Preferences: Add crumbled feta cheese or toasted nuts for extra texture.

Golden Grilled Cheese

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 2 slices of sourdough bread, 2 slices of cheddar cheese, 1 tbsp butter

Instructions: Butter the outside of each bread slice. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Grill sandwich on both sides until golden brown and the cheese is melted. Cut diagonally and serve immediately for that perfect cheese pull.

Notes & Preferences: Add a slice of tomato or a sprinkle of chili flakes for a twist.

Rice Pudding

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients: Rice, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon

Instructions: Cook rice until soft, then add milk and sugar. Simmer and stir until thick. Add vanilla and cinnamon before serving.

Notes: Can be served warm or chilled.

Mint Lemon Drink

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients: Fresh mint leaves, lemon juice, sugar, water, ice

Instructions: Blend mint with lemon juice, sugar, and water. Serve over ice and garnish with mint leaves.

Notes & Preferences: A refreshing drink ideal for warm weather.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: 250g spaghetti, 200g ground beef, 1 onion (finely chopped), 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 cup tomato sauce, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, black pepper, dried oregano

Instructions: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook spaghetti until al dente. Meanwhile, in a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce, season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer for 10 minutes. Drain pasta, mix with the sauce, and serve hot with grated parmesan on top.

Notes & Preferences: For a touch of freshness, garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
