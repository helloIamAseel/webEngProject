Our Favorite Recipes

Spaghetti Bolognese

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: 250g spaghetti, 200g ground beef, 1 onion (finely chopped), 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 cup tomato sauce, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, black pepper, dried oregano

Instructions: Cook spaghetti in boiling water until tender. In a separate pan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, then add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce, season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer briefly, combine with spaghetti, and serve warm.

Notes: For a touch of freshness, garnish with chopped parsley before serving.

Crisp Garden Salad

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 2 cups romaine lettuce (chopped), 1 cucumber (sliced), 1 tomato (diced), ¼ red onion (thinly sliced), 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt, black pepper

Instructions: In a salad bowl, combine all chopped vegetables. In a small jar, shake olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to emulsify. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss just before serving for a fresh, vibrant taste.

Notes & Preferences: Add crumbled feta cheese or toasted nuts for extra texture.

Buttermilk Pancakes

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients: 1½ cups flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, ½ tsp baking soda, pinch of salt, 1 egg, 1¼ cups buttermilk, 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions: In a bowl, whisk all dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, beat egg with buttermilk and melted butter. Combine wet and dry mixtures gently—do not overmix. Heat a non-stick skillet, pour ¼ cup of batter per pancake. Flip once bubbles form. Serve with maple syrup, berries, or a pat of butter.

Notes & Preferences: Let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking for extra fluffiness.

Baked Chicken Tenders

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: Chicken breast strips, breadcrumbs, olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder

Instructions: Coat chicken with seasoning and breadcrumbs. Arrange on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, and bake until golden and crispy.

Notes: Healthier than frying, and great for quick meals or snacks.

French Toast

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients: Sliced bread, eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, butter

Instructions: Whisk eggs with milk, cinnamon, and vanilla. Dip bread slices and cook on a buttered skillet until golden brown on both sides. Serve with honey or fruit.

Notes: Perfect for weekend breakfasts or simple sweet cravings.
