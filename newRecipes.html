Baked Chicken Tenders

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: Chicken breast strips, breadcrumbs, olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder

Instructions: Coat chicken with seasoning and breadcrumbs. Arrange on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, and bake until golden and crispy.

Notes: Healthier than frying, and great for quick meals or snacks.

French Toast

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients: Sliced bread, eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, butter

Instructions: Whisk eggs with milk, cinnamon, and vanilla. Dip bread slices and cook on a buttered skillet until golden brown on both sides. Serve with honey or fruit.

Notes: Perfect for weekend breakfasts or simple sweet cravings.

Cucumber Yogurt Dip

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: Grated cucumber, plain yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, dried mint, salt

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Chill before serving. Enjoy as a dip with bread or vegetables.

Notes: Cool, refreshing, and quick to prepare.

Rice Pudding

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients: Rice, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon

Instructions: Cook rice until soft, then add milk and sugar. Simmer and stir until thick. Add vanilla and cinnamon before serving.

Notes: Can be served warm or chilled.
