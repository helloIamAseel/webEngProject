Berry Yogurt Smoothie

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients: 1 banana, ½ cup strawberries, ½ cup blueberries, 1 cup plain yogurt, 1 tbsp honey, ½ cup cold milk or almond milk

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blend until silky smooth. Pour into a chilled glass and enjoy immediately. Perfect for breakfast or a refreshing mid-day boost.

Notes & Preferences: Freeze the fruits beforehand for an extra cold, milkshake-like texture.

Mint Lemon Drink

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients: Fresh mint leaves, lemon juice, sugar, water, ice

Instructions: Blend mint with lemon juice, sugar, and water. Serve over ice and garnish with mint leaves.

Notes & Preferences: A refreshing drink ideal for warm weather.

Chickpea Curry

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients: Chickpeas, chopped tomatoes, onion, garlic, ginger, coconut milk, curry powder, salt, black pepper

Instructions: Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger. Add tomatoes and chickpeas, then stir in coconut milk and spices. Simmer until thickened. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Notes: A rich, plant-based dish full of flavor and warmth.

Baked Chicken Tenders

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: Chicken breast strips, breadcrumbs, olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder

Instructions: Coat chicken with seasoning and breadcrumbs. Arrange on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, and bake until golden and crispy.

Notes: Healthier than frying, and great for quick meals or snacks.

French Toast

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients: Sliced bread, eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, butter

Instructions: Whisk eggs with milk, cinnamon, and vanilla. Dip bread slices and cook on a buttered skillet until golden brown on both sides. Serve with honey or fruit.

Notes: Perfect for weekend breakfasts or simple sweet cravings.

Cucumber Yogurt Dip

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: Grated cucumber, plain yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, dried mint, salt

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Chill before serving. Enjoy as a dip with bread or vegetables.

Notes: Cool, refreshing, and quick to prepare.
