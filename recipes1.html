Spaghetti Bolognese

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: 250g spaghetti, 200g ground beef, 1 onion (finely chopped), 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 cup tomato sauce, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, black pepper, dried oregano

Instructions: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook spaghetti until al dente. Meanwhile, in a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce, season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer for 10 minutes. Drain pasta, mix with the sauce, and serve hot with grated parmesan on top.

Notes & Preferences:For a touch of freshness, garnish with chopped parsley before serving.

Buttermilk Pancakes

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients: 1½ cups flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, ½ tsp baking soda, pinch of salt, 1 egg, 1¼ cups buttermilk, 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions: In a bowl, whisk all dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, beat egg with buttermilk and melted butter. Combine wet and dry mixtures gently—do not overmix. Heat a non-stick skillet, pour ¼ cup of batter per pancake. Flip once bubbles form. Serve with maple syrup, berries, or a pat of butter.

Notes & Preferences: Let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking for extra fluffiness.

Crisp Garden Salad

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 2 cups romaine lettuce (chopped), 1 cucumber (sliced), 1 tomato (diced), ¼ red onion (thinly sliced), 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt, black pepper

Instructions: In a salad bowl, combine all chopped vegetables. In a small jar, shake olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to emulsify. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss just before serving for a fresh, vibrant taste.

Notes & Preferences: Add crumbled feta cheese or toasted nuts for extra texture.

Hearty Chicken Soup

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients: 2 chicken breasts, 2 carrots (chopped), 2 celery stalks (sliced), 1 onion (diced), 3 garlic cloves (smashed), 1 bay leaf, 5 cups chicken broth, salt, pepper, fresh parsley

Instructions: In a pot, bring chicken broth to a boil. Add all vegetables, bay leaf, and chicken. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through (30–35 mins). Remove chicken, shred it, and return it to the soup. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley. Serve warm with bread on the side.

Notes & Preferences: Use bone-in chicken for deeper flavor, and remove bones before serving.

Golden Grilled Cheese

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 2 slices of sourdough bread, 2 slices of cheddar cheese, 1 tbsp butter

Instructions: Butter the outside of each bread slice. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Grill sandwich on both sides until golden brown and the cheese is melted. Cut diagonally and serve immediately for that perfect cheese pull.

Notes & Preferences: Add a slice of tomato or a sprinkle of chili flakes for a twist.

Berry Yogurt Smoothie

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients: 1 banana, ½ cup strawberries, ½ cup blueberries, 1 cup plain yogurt, 1 tbsp honey, ½ cup cold milk or almond milk

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blend until silky smooth. Pour into a chilled glass and enjoy immediately. Perfect for breakfast or a refreshing mid-day boost.

Notes & Preferences: Freeze the fruits beforehand for an extra cold, milkshake-like texture.

Mint Lemon Drink

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients: Fresh mint leaves, lemon juice, sugar, water, ice

Instructions: Blend mint with lemon juice, sugar, and water. Serve over ice and garnish with mint leaves.

Notes & Preferences: A refreshing drink ideal for warm weather.

Chickpea Curry

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients: Chickpeas, chopped tomatoes, onion, garlic, ginger, coconut milk, curry powder, salt, black pepper

Instructions: Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger. Add tomatoes and chickpeas, then stir in coconut milk and spices. Simmer until thickened. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Notes: A rich, plant-based dish full of flavor and warmth.

Baked Chicken Tenders

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients: Chicken breast strips, breadcrumbs, olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder

Instructions: Coat chicken with seasoning and breadcrumbs. Arrange on a baking tray, drizzle with olive oil, and bake until golden and crispy.

Notes: Healthier than frying, and great for quick meals or snacks.

French Toast

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients: Sliced bread, eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, butter

Instructions: Whisk eggs with milk, cinnamon, and vanilla. Dip bread slices and cook on a buttered skillet until golden brown on both sides. Serve with honey or fruit.

Notes: Perfect for weekend breakfasts or simple sweet cravings.

Cucumber Yogurt Dip

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: Grated cucumber, plain yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, dried mint, salt

Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Chill before serving. Enjoy as a dip with bread or vegetables.

Notes: Cool, refreshing, and quick to prepare.
